Sistema de Scoring
How points are calculated in each phase of the tournament
🏘 How knockout bracket predictions work
In the knockout stage you don't predict specific teams, but bracket positions. Each match is pre-defined — por ejemplo, "2º Grupo A vs 1º Grupo B". Sea quien sea quien quede en esas posiciones, ese partido se jugará.
🏂 Round of 32
Ej: 2º Grupo A vs 1º Grupo B
The match is played between whoever finishes 2nd in Group A and 1st in Group B, regardless of which team it is.
→ Subsequent rounds
The winner of each match advances to the next pre-defined bracket slot
As the tournament progresses, slots are filled by winners from the corresponding side.
⚠️ What if the match no se juega con los equipos que pronostiqué?
The match always takes place between whoever qualified for those positions. Your points depend on who wins that specific match, not which exact teams are in it.
🔎 Example: the “reversed” final
You predict “Spain vs Portugal” in the final. Si el cuadro los pone en sentido inverso aparece “Portugal vs Spain” — es el mismo partido. Si Portugal gana, puntúas igual. Solo se da un problema si ambos quedan en el mismo lado del cuadro y se eliminan entre sí antes de la final — en ese caso simplemente no puntúas ese partido.

⚡ Fase Eliminatoria (Desde Round of 32)
In knockouts there can be a draw after 90' with qualification via penalties. The system rewards in layers: who advances, correct 1/X/2, and exact score.
The team you predicted advances
Regardless of the score
+2 pts

Home win, draw (with qualification), or away win
+1 pt
Exact score
El resultado exacto en goles coincide
+3 pts
Maximum per match (advance + 1X2 + exact)
+6 pts
